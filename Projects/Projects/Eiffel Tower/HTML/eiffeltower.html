The Eiffel Tower

What/Where is the Eiffel Tower?

The Eiffel Tower or La Tour Eiffel is a historical building located in Paris, France. The tower is 300 meters (984 feet) high and has 3 levels. This Eiffel Tower was designed by Gustave Eiffel, an engineer who also helped build the Statue of Liberty in New York! On January 26, 1887, construction for the Eiffel Tower had begun. The tower was finished on March 31st 1889, which was a record time back then! The Eiffel Tower is a tourist attraction that many travellers go to because of its beauty and popularity.

The History of the Eiffel Tower

In celebration of the 100th year anniversary of the French Revolution, also called the 1889 Exposition Universelle, a competition was launched. The French government wanted a monument in honour of the 1889 Exposition Universelle. Therefore, a competition was held for designs of a potential monument with the following criteria:

  1. Must be made with iron
  2. Have a square base
  3. Be 300 meters tall

More than 100 plans were submitted, however Gustave Eiffel's design was selected.

Why is the Eiffel Tower so Popular

The Eiffel Tower is so popular as it is a symbol of France. Back when it was first built, people were amazed by it because of its design. The tower has also contributed to technical advancements in France. The antenna at the top of the Eiffel Tower broadcast all television and radio channels to 12 million people living in the Île de France.

There are also many attractions on the Eiffel Tower like the restaurants and gift shops. You are able to eat food while enjoying the views from the Eiffel Tower and you can buy something to remember your trip! There are 4 restaurants located on the Eiffel Tower…

  • Madame Brasserie (on first floor)
  • The Jules Verne (on second floor)
  • The Buffets (on first and second floor)
  • The Champagne Bar (at the top)

The Eiffel Tower Now

Over the years, the Eiffel Tower has received renovations to keep up with current safety regulations. The Eiffel Tower also sparkles at night for 5 minutes every hour! This monument has become one of the most famous buildings of all of France and is on most people's vacation lists!

Fun Facts about the Eiffel Tower

  • The Eiffel Tower welcomes over 7 million visitors a year! 75% of those visitors are travellers.
  • There are 1,665 stairs on the Eiffel Tower. However we can only go on 674 of them for safety reasons.
  • With the TDF antenna on the Eiffel Tower, the tower measures to 330 meters!
